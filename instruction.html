Instructions for the game:

Flappy bird the game is classic game. I have created it in 3 levels. You should make sure to not hit the bird to a obstacle.


1.Tap the arrow to start. Tap the screen again to allow your bird to fly and to start the game.
2.Stay in the middle of screen until the first set of pipes appears. Measure your tap heights to go between the two pipes.
3.Count the record and then score.
4.Medium level game is coming some fruits how to take the fruits in the bird.
5.Master level game is coming some enemies how to you manage with enemies and go to the goal
